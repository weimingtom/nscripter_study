delay

delay <number>

Causes a delay of specified time (in milliseconds) that cannot be escaped out of by clicking. While this is the same as the special character 'd', in this case you can supply a variable as an argument.

delay

delay <数字>

数字ミリ秒分だけ、待ちます。クリックで飛ばせます。
特殊表示文字dと同じですが、変数を引数に使えます。
