nsa

Allows for NSA archive mode. In this mode, resources such as BMPs may be read from nsa*.arc, located in the same directory as the executable itself. The nsa*.arc files themselves are generated by nsaarc.exe, which is found in the SDK.

Please note that you may consecutively name files arc.nsa arc1.nsa arc2.nsa～arc9.nsa, and they will all for the purposes of the interpreter be considered to be part of the same namespace -- as if it is one big archive. You may use this to quickly unpackage and repackage archives for testing.

nsa

请注意你可以连续地命名文件arc.nsa、arc1.nsa、arc2.nsa～arc9.nsa， 而他们将全部被当作相同名字空间的一部分作为解释器的目的——就像他们是一个大的存档文件。 你可以使用这个规则快速地解包和重新封包存档文件以供测试。
